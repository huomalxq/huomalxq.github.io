  <div class="study-principles">
    <p class="section-kicker">Writing Rules</p>
    <h2>记录原则</h2>
    <div class="principle-grid">
      <article class="principle-card">
        <strong>单页单问题</strong>
        <p>一篇笔记围绕一个核心问题展开，避免把多个不相关结论塞进同一页。</p>
      </article>
      <article class="principle-card">
        <strong>分类页做导航</strong>
        <p>分类页负责说明主题边界和笔记索引，细节、命令和实验结果放进独立页面。</p>
      </article>
      <article class="principle-card">
        <strong>标题要可搜索</strong>
        <p>标题写清对象、动作和场景，例如“LangGraph Text2SQL 节点重试设计”。</p>
      </article>
    </div>
